# Sprator — 90-second demo script

**Setup before recording:**
- Screen split: **Telegram (left)** with your Hermes chat open · **sprator.vercel.app (right)**, browser zoom ~80%.
- Have these three messages ready to paste into Telegram, in order:
  1. `audit this month`
  2. `approve Figma and Zoom, hold AWS`
  3. `provision Anthropic API credits at $40/mo`
- Refresh the dashboard once right before you hit record (so the Net Impact banner is fresh).

---

### 0:00–0:12 — HOOK (dashboard on screen)
**DO:** Show the dashboard, cursor on the green **Net Agent Impact** banner.
**SAY:** "This is Sprator — an autonomous cashflow agent. It's run by my own Hermes agent, over Telegram. And it does both sides of the job: it *recovers* revenue and it *controls* spend. Right now it's realized four thousand dollars for me — automatically."

### 0:12–0:35 — AUDIT + GUARDRAILS (switch focus to Telegram)
**DO:** Paste `audit this month`. Let Hermes reply.
**SAY:** "I just ask it to audit the month. Watch what it does on its own — it *auto-cancelled* Notion AI, because that's under my twenty-five-dollar guardrail. The bigger ones — Figma, Zoom, an AWS anomaly — it *escalates* to me instead of acting alone."
**DO:** Cursor traces the "auto-handled" vs "needs approval" split in Hermes's reply.

### 0:35–0:50 — HUMAN APPROVAL (Telegram → dashboard)
**DO:** Paste `approve Figma and Zoom, hold AWS`.
**SAY:** "I approve the two I trust, and tell it to hold the AWS one for me to investigate."
**DO:** Glance to the dashboard — point at the audit trail / activity feed updating.
**SAY:** "Every action is logged with a Stripe reference. Full audit trail."

### 0:50–1:12 — AUTONOMOUS SPEND (the money shot)
**DO:** Paste `provision Anthropic API credits at $40/mo`. When Hermes returns the link, click it — let the **real Stripe checkout page** open.
**SAY:** "And it doesn't just cut costs — it can *spend*. I tell it to provision its own API credits, and it pays for them through Stripe — a real payment, inside its monthly cap. The agent just bought something it needed, on its own, safely."

### 1:12–1:30 — CLOSE (back to dashboard)
**DO:** Return to the Net Impact banner + guardrail chips.
**SAY:** "Earns, spends, and stays inside its guardrails — auto-approve under twenty-five, hard cap at two thousand. That's Sprator: an autonomous CFO, built on Hermes, Stripe, and NVIDIA. Thanks."
